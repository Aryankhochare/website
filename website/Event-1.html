DON BOSCO COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, FATORDA – GOA
IEEE STUDENT BRANCH – STB 11433




INAUGURAL REPORT



The Don Bosco College of Engineering, Fatorda, Goa established an IEEE student branch consisting of 74 students with the help of Prof. Yeshudas Muttu as the IEEE Branch Counselor, DBCE. The inaugural ceremony held on 1st February 2019 in the Seminar Hall of the college. The Chief Guest for the function was Mr. Shashank Krishna; Director & CEO of Katmal Info Technology, Padma Shri 2019 Nominee (Science and Engineering field) and the Guest of honor was Dr. B. Satyanarayana, IEEE Bombay section Secretary and Scientific Officer (H), TIFR, Mumbai.

The compere for the inaugural ceremony was IEEE Student Chair, Mr. Sherwin Colaco. The function commenced with the unveiling of the logos of both the student branches designed by Mr. Siddesh Nayak, followed by lighting of the traditional lamp. The dignitaries were then given a floral welcome. The Director of DBCE, Rev. Fr. Kinley D’Cruz motivated everyone with inspiring and encouraging words in his welcome speech. Dr. Neena S. P. Panandikar, Principal of DBCE introduced the Chief Guest and the Guest of Honor of the function and congratulated Prof. Yeshudas Muttu for their efforts and support. She also appreciated Dr. Varsha Turkar, the Advisor of IEEE student branch for taking this initiative & providing her constant support and guidance.
Prof. Yeshudas Muttu introduced the gathering to the world’s largest technical professional organization, IEEE: Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers, stating its importance with growing technology, about its various facilities and support to the aspiring minds followed by a short note on Student Branch formation. The IEEE Student Branch Officers were later introduced and the Guest of Honor, Dr. B. Satyanarayana presented each member with their respective badges. The Branch Officers appointed are Mr. Sherwin Colaco as the Chairperson, Mr. Rohan Bukkam as the Vice Chair, Mr. Rajat Metry as the Treasurer, Mr. Shivam Bale as Joint Treasurer, Ms. Juella De Oliveira as the Secretary and Mr. Raj Kubal as the Joint Secretary of IEEE student branch of DBCE. Later, the oath was taken by the Executive Committee of IEEE Student Branch, DBCE.
This was followed by the vote of thanks. The function concluded with the premiere of the IEEE DBCE student Branch video.

IEEE STUDENT BRANCH – STB 11433




IEEE Awareness Programme & IEEE EX – CO: Duties & Responsibilities



IEEE Awareness Program was held for students of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering & Computer Engineering by Prof. Yeshudas Muttu on 23 rd & 24 th August 2018. The main purpose of Session was to make students aware about the Professional body like IEEE & its importance in engineering field. Formation of IEEE student’s branch was the main focus of the session. Various benefits of IEEE student’s branch in our college were discussed. The students were made aware about activities like Seminars, Hands-on Workshops, Industrial Field visits, expert lectures and technical events to be organized under the student’s branch for students as well as the faculty members on and off campus that helps in skill development & lifelong learning. Importance of professional networking, development of leadership qualities, team spirit & research culture amongst students are some of the major highlights of the seminar that will lead to technological innovation and excellence with social benefits. A month later, we had about 50 IEEE members registered.

The session on “IEEE EX – CO: Duties & Responsibilities” was held on 5 th October 2018 delivered by IEEE branch counselor: Prof. Yeshudas Muttu & IEEE student Members: Mr. Sherwin Colaco & Mr. Rohan Bukkam. The session began with members to be elected & their role in IEEE student branch. Mr. Sherwin Colaco explained the role of Chair Person & Secretary. Mr. Rohan Bukkam described the qualities & roles of Vice Chair & Treasurer. Prof. Yeshudas Muttu also mentioned that besides the core EX – CO, other sub – committees can also be formed like Membership committee, Financial committee, Publicity Committee & Program Committee. The session was followed by Secret – ballot elections & Core IEEE EX – CO committee was formed.

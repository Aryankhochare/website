Women in Engineering


IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. This global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. It is the largest international professional organization dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to career engineering, now stands at the top most position, for which each one of you being part of it can be proud of.


IEEE DBCE Student Branch WIE Affinity Group got approved on August 7, 2020 & its geo code is SBA 11433. Its main objective is to make lifelong amities, acquire powerful mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.
